# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Welcome different viewpoints
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private security/contact issue as appropriate.
